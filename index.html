About Tony

Tony is a BBQ and fried chicken aficionado who works as a full-stack web developer in the Chicago area. When he isn't coding, Tony might be found playing pick up basketball or taking in a concert with friends. He also enjoys noodling on guitar and making homemade sausages.

Tony graduated from the University of Dayton in 2007 with a BS in Physics and spent time teaching before transitioning to a role as a software implementation consultant. He learned to code at the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp in 2017. Tony is always interested in finding fresh ways to solve problems and loves that web development affords him with the opportunity to learn something new each day.
